About

I'm Paul Seigneur III — a sound designer, musician, voice actor, and singer with 9 years of deep, self-directed practice in binaural audio, immersive sound design, and audio-first storytelling. I work across the full audio stack: I can design the soundscape, write and produce the music, perform the voice, and mix it all down in Reaper.

I also work part-time as an accessibility tester at the University of Colorado Boulder, auditing software, apps, and digital content for real-world usability with screen readers and assistive technology — the same expertise I bring to accessibility-focused audio work.

I'm blind, and that shapes the way I approach sound. Where a sighted designer reaches for a visual, I reach for depth, placement, and texture. It makes me especially well-suited to work where the audio has to carry the experience: audio dramas, accessibility-focused games, screen-reader UX, and anything meant to be heard through headphones rather than watched.

I work independently and I'm open to collaborations, contract work, voice gigs, and full-time roles.
